DawgPool Project

This app, created at UGAHacks8, makes travel easier for students by connecting them with other students going the same way, allowing them to carpool and save money while reducing their carbon footprint. The intuitive interface lets drivers post rides and riders browse and choose the best option to travel safely and build connections.
